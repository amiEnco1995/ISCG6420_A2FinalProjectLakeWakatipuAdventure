About Us

Established in 2008 by two seafaring couple, Hanna and James. Join our family friendly adventure around Lake Wakatipu. We have informative tour guides, complimentary sunscreen and hats if you ask for it and better yet an in-house chef preparing all the meals at our on board Blue Water Cafe. So come along and get to know Queenstown's favorite Lake.
